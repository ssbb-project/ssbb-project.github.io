# SSBB NDO & AMP Database

Welcome! This site hosts an open-access database of **non-digestible oligosaccharides (NDOs)** and **antimicrobial peptides (AMPs)** collected by the *Stop Spread Bad Bugs (SSBB)* MSCA consortium.

<Callout type="info" title="About the Project">
  The SSBB project aims to advance the understanding of NDOs and AMPs and their potential to combat antimicrobial resistance.
</Callout>

Explore the datasets, structural information, and related documentation using the navigation sidebar.
