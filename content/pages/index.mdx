# SSBB NDO & AMP Database

Welcome! This site hosts an open-access database of **non-digestible oligosaccharides (NDOs)** and **antimicrobial peptides (AMPs)** collected by the *Stop Spread Bad Bugs (SSBB)* MSCA consortium.

> **About the Project**  
> A publicly accessible library of NDO and AMP structures with related experimental data.
